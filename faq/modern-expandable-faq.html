Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform.

How does the 3D rendering engine work?
Our engine utilizes WebGL and custom shaders to provide real-time hardware acceleration, ensuring smooth performance even with thousands of dynamic particles.
Can I customize the glassmorphism effects?
Yes! Every component comes with exposed CSS variables for blur intensity, border opacity, and background tint, allowing for complete design flexibility.
What is the licensing for these components?
All UI elements are provided under the MIT License, meaning you can use them for both personal and commercial projects without any restrictions.
Are the components mobile-friendly?
Every element is built with a mobile-first approach, using responsive units and fluid layouts to ensure a perfect experience across all device sizes.
