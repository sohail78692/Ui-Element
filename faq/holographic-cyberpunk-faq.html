NEURAL FAQ

What is Neural Link Integration?
Neural Link Integration allows for direct biological connection between the user's cerebral cortex and the mainframe, enabling data transfer rates up to 100 TB/s with minimal synaptic latency.
Is the holographic interface safe?
Yes, all holographic emissions are filtered through UV-blockers and meet standard safety protocols. Minor retinal flickering may occur during the calibration phase.
How do I reset my biometric key?
Biometric keys can be reset at any authorized district terminal using your original DNA sequence or a verified neural signature backup.
